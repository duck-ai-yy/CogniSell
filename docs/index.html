The Pain

Why industrial sales teams lose deals they should win

Long-cycle B2B sales create four systemic failures that no traditional CRM solves.

📧

Context Fragmentation

Andreas promised a conveyor RFQ by month-end. That commitment lives in email #47 of a 3-month thread. Your CRM has "follow up" with no context. When your colleague covers for you, they start from zero.

💤

Silent Relationship Decay

You met Markus at Hannover Messe 92 days ago. No system flagged it. By the time you remember, he's already talking to your competitor. The relationship cooled — silently, with no alert.

🔍

Black-Box AI Memory

AI CRMs stuff everything into vector embeddings. "The system knows." But what does it know? How confident is it? Who told it? You can't audit a vector. You can't correct a cosine similarity.

🔄

Missed Signals

Henrik changed jobs last week — from a prospect company to a buying decision-maker. A tender was announced. The signals were there. Nobody was watching.

The Shift

From transaction logging to cognitive auditing

CogniSell doesn't just record what happened — it tracks what the system believes, how confident it is, and whether a human has verified it.

Product Features

What makes CogniSell different

Every feature is a direct answer to one of the pain points above. No feature exists "just because."

Core

Auditable Knowledge Graph

Every relationship fact is a triple with source, extractor, confidence, and cognitive status. No vectors — every fact is human-readable. You can always answer: "Why does the system believe this?"

(subject) —[predicate]→ (object)
confidence: 0.83 | status: confirmed
UX

Decision Inbox

Agents never block on humans. Low-confidence or high-risk facts surface as cards — one click to confirm, correct, or dismiss. High-confidence facts auto-confirm silently. The more you clear, the less it asks.

high_conf + low_risk → auto_confirm
low_conf OR high_risk → inbox_card
AI

Multi-Agent Strategy Debate

Three sub-agents debate the best approach angle: Champion (aggressive pitch), Skeptic (compliance risks), Closer (converge). Fixed rounds, structured output — LLMs fill arguments, scripts control flow.

Champion → Skeptic → Closer
N rounds, converge to strategy_card
Ops

Relationship Decay Detection

Automatic 90-day sweep marks cold contacts. Job changes trigger instant red-dot alerts. Catch-up suggestions are grounded in graph context — not generic templates. The graph remembers so you don't have to.

decay_scan(now) → stale_edges
→ catch_up_card + context
AI

Auto-Extraction Pipeline

Business cards → OCR → GLiNER2 entity extraction. Email threads → commitment and next-step extraction. Every extracted fact enters as "proposed" — nothing gets confirmed without a human or a high-confidence threshold.

GLiNER2: card_fields + email_triples
→ proposed edges → sync_engine
Core

Hot-Pluggable Skill Registry

Each agent composes skills from a runtime registry. Plug in a new skill → the agent gains a new capability instantly. Demo it live: add lead.enrich, watch the graph grow new edges in real time.

skills/registry → register(skill)
→ agent.capabilities.append(skill)

Demo Flow

One lead, end to end

Follow Andreas Vogel — Head of Procurement at Stahlwerk Nord GmbH — from business card to ongoing relationship.

1

Scan Business Card

Upload Andreas's card from Hannover Messe. Agents stream OCR + extraction progress. Proposed nodes appear with dashed borders.

2

Confirm in Decision Inbox

Low-confidence fields surface as cards. Click to confirm → edges go solid. The graph knows what's verified.

3

Enrich & Strategize

Background enrichment pulls company data. Three-role debate produces approach strategies. Pick one — preferences remembered.

4

Draft & Send Email

Cold email drafted from strategy context. Review, edit, send. Reply arrives → digest extracts new commitments → graph grows.

5

Decay Detection

90-day sweep finds cold contacts — nodes turn grey. Catch-up suggestions grounded in graph context, not templates.

6

Social monitor detects Henrik's new role. Red-dot notification appears. Click to review, act, auto-dismiss.
